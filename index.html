About the tournament
This year’s tournament and toy drive benefits the Phoenix Children's Hospital. Ames is committed to raising awareness for children in need.The Ames golf tournament is a 4-person scramble with hole activities, lunch buffet, and an awards reception. Sponsorship opportunities are available, and all net proceeds from the event provide immediate support to Phoenix Children’s Hospital. Please check back closer to the date for an event schedule.


Philanthropy is critical to Phoenix Children's Hospital. In fact, more than 60 of their programs and services are funded solely or significantly through charitable gifts. Many of the programs that rely on philantrhopy are those that make the hospital more comfortable for their young patients -- pet therapy, onsite school, Child Life, and the Emily Center Library.

Specifically, your gifts help them: fund groundbreaking research, launch new clinical programs, recruit world-renowned physicians, provide state-of-the-art technology and equipment, offer child-friendly programs, and treat each child in need.

About Phoenix Children's Hospital
Phoenix Children’s Hospital is Arizona’s only children’s hospital recognized by U.S. News & World Report’s Best Children’s Hospitals with rankings in all ten specialties. Phoenix Children’s provides world-class inpatient, outpatient, trauma, emergency and urgent care to children and families in Arizona and throughout the Southwest. As one of the largest children’s hospitals in the country, Phoenix Children’s provides care across more than 75 pediatric specialties. The Hospital is poised for continued growth in quality patient care, research and medical education.

For more information about the hospital visit http://www.phoenixchildrens.org.
